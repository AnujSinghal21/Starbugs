About Us

Health++ serves as an all-in-one automated platform for accessing the facilities provided by the Health Centre at IIT Kanpur. The Health Centre is dedicated to improving the health and wellbeing of the campus community through top-notch prevention, education, and treatment services. Their medical, counseling, and health promotion services are designed to help individuals maintain good health or recover from illnesses or injuries, thereby minimizing disruptions.

The Health Centre strives to enhance the healthcare experience of the IITK community by providing healthcare services that are respectful, considerate, and confidential. To learn more about available healthcare resources, preventive measures, health education, and general healthcare guidance, please visit the website or speak with Health Centre staff. Our aim is to offer a user-friendly platform for communication between hardworking Health Centre staff and students.

If you have questions about the functionalities of Health++, or if you encounter any issues, please don't hesitate to reach out to the development team.
